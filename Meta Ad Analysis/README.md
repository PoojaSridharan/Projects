## Project Description: 

This repository contains a Power BI project designed to analyze and visualize Meta (Facebook and Instagram) advertisement data. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of campaign efficiency by tracking key performance indicators (KPIs) such as Click-Through Rate (CTR), conversion rates, and demographic engagement across global markets.

## Key Features:

* KPI Scorecard: Real-time tracking of Impressions (340K), Clicks (40K), and Purchases (2K).

* Demographic Analysis: Visualization of engagement by age group and gender, identifying the 20â€“30 age bracket as a primary driver.

* Temporal Trends: Analysis of engagement by week and specific "Time of the Day" segments (Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night).

* Global Reach: Geographical breakdown of performance across North America, Europe, Asia, Africa, and South America.

* Creative Analysis: Comparison of performance metrics across Ad Types including Video, Image, Stories, and Carousel.

## Technical Specifications:

* Tool: Power BI Desktop.

* Data Source: Kaggle.

## Key Metrics Calculated:

* CTR: 11.79% 

* Engagement Rate: 13.58% 

* Conversion Rate: 5.07% 

* Purchase Rate: 0.60% 


## Interactive Filters: 
Users can slice data by Campaign Name, Target Interest, Month, and specific Engagement levels.

## Dashboard Insights

* Peak Engagement: The "Afternoon" period recorded the highest engagement at 11,610 interactions.

* Gender Split: "All" gender categories led engagement at 41.05%, followed by Female (35.45%) and Male (23.5%).

* Financial Scope: Managed a total budget of $2.54M with an average campaign spend of $50.72K.
